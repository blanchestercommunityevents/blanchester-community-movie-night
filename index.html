This event is open to families, friends, neighbors, and visitors from Blanchester and surrounding communities. Bring the people you care about and enjoy a relaxing summer evening together.

🪑 What to Bring

  • Lawn chairs
  • Blankets
  • Jackets or sweatshirts for later in the evening
  • Bug spray, if desired
  • Friends, family, and neighbors
  • Your own additional snacks, if desired

Seating is not provided, so please bring a lawn chair or blanket for each person in your group.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the event really free?

Yes. Admission, food, drinks, activities, and popcorn are free while supplies last.

Do I need to register?

No registration is required. Just bring your family and come enjoy the evening.

Who is invited?

Everyone is welcome, including families, friends, neighbors, and visitors from nearby communities.

What time does the movie begin?

Food and activities begin at 7:00 PM. The movie will begin at dusk, once it is dark enough to see the screen clearly.

Can I bring my own snacks?

Yes. You are welcome to bring additional snacks and nonalcoholic drinks for your family.

What happens if there is bad weather?

Any weather-related updates or schedule changes will be shared before the event.

Is seating provided?

No. Please bring lawn chairs or blankets for your group.
